Concentration

This game will not work in your browser. Please switch to a browser that supports advanced features icluding CSS 3D transforms, such as Google Chrome.

This is the classic game of Concentration aka Memory. You have to match people's faces to their names. Start the game by clicking on a card to turn it over. Only two cards can be flipped over at a time. If the flipped over cards match they will disappear. The goal is to make the cards disappear as fast as possible.

For your privacy and security these contacts are never sent to our servers, they are only stored on your computer.

These pictures are taken from their public profile pictures on social networks and may not neceasrily be of their faces.

To use larger pictures, or to try out the LinkedIn or Google Contacts version of the game, go to the main game page.

Good luck!
