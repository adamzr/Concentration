Concentration

This is the classic game of Concentration aka Memory. You have to match people's faces to their names. When you click the Play button you will be asked to authorize Google Contacts to allow us to view your contacts. For your privacy and security reasons these contacts are never sent to our servers, they are only stored on your computer. We use them to populate the deck with the names and faces of your friends using RainMaker. These pictures are taken from their public profile pictures on social networks and may not neceasrily be of their faces. Good luck! Play!
